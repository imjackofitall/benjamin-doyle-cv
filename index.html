Personal Profile

As a software engineer, I enjoy progressing code, ideas, and team culture. I keep my skills broad and enjoy working across multiple programming languages. I consider myself a pragmatic, all-round product engineer who adapts to the technical stack a project requires, and only seeks change when there is a clear need.

As a leader, I strongly advocate for team culture and collaboration. I believe in a flat hierarchy of ideas and high levels of cross-team and cross-discipline collaboration, with each discipline in the product trio having a clear voice. I am confident in public speaking and teaching, with extensive experience running technical and cultural interviews across Australia and with distributed international teams.

I am experienced in implementing and adapting agile practices to fit the team and the business, rather than forcing teams to fit a process. I treat process changes like experiments: getting genuine buy-in first, running one change at a time, and measuring whether it actually helps. I know when to push for improvement and when to say "not yet" to good ideas that come at the wrong time.

I put a lot of effort into the work I do, but it does not define me.
